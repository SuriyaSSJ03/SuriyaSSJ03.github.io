About Me

I'm Suriya Jaisankar, a passionate video editor, motion graphics artist, and content creator with **3.5 years of dedicated experience** in bringing visual narratives to life. I've had the privilege of working with a diverse range of clients, from dynamic **YouTube creators** to leading **agencies** and prominent **brands**.
